Fried Chicken

Description:

Crispy and juicy fried chicken with a golden brown crust. This classic recipe will satisfy your comfort food cravings.


Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Chicken: Rinse the chicken pieces under cold water and pat dry with paper towels.
  2. Prepare Breading: In a large bowl, combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using).
  3. Prepare Wet Mix: In another bowl, whisk together eggs and buttermilk.
  4. Coat Chicken: Dip each chicken piece into the wet mix, then dredge in the flour mixture, ensuring a thorough coating.
  5. Heat Oil: In a large skillet or deep fryer, heat vegetable oil to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Fry Chicken: Fry the chicken pieces in batches, without overcrowding, until golden brown and cooked through, about 8-10 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve: Serve hot and enjoy with your favorite sides.
